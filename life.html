Rules: Click on a cell in the grid to make it alive. Red cells are alive and white cells are dead.

In every generation, each cell interacts with its adjacent cells whether it be horizonal, vertical, or diagonal.

1. A live cell with less than two live neighbours dies

2. A live cell with two or three neighbours lives on

3. A live cell with more than three neighbours dies

4. A dead cell with exactly three neighbours comes alive

All cells interact with each other concurrently.

After you click start, life begins.
